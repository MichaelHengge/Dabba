What is Dabba?

Dabba is inspired by the traditional Masala Dabba, the iconic Indian spice box that keeps essential ingredients organized and accessible in every Indian kitchen.

Just like the Masala Dabba stores spices, our Dabba is a digital database designed to help you store, search, and manage all your kitchen ingredients efficiently. Whether you're tracking pantry staples, dietary restrictions, or allergens, Dabba keeps everything in one place.

Use Dabba to ensure your kitchen is always stocked with what you need, reducing waste and making cooking easier!
